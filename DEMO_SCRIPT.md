# Brainiac — Demo Script
### Hackathon Pitch · Conversational Voiceover

---

## HOOK (0:00 – 0:12)

*Show a grid of Discord and Telegram notification icons piling up*

> "You're in 20 alpha groups. But realistically? You only have time to check two or three of them each day. And that's where opportunity walks right past you."

---

## THE PROBLEM (0:12 – 0:35)

*Cut to a phone screen flooded with hundreds of unread messages*

> "Think about what you're missing. A wallet drainer warning in a server you haven't opened in a week. A mint that filled in three hours — announced in a Telegram chat you forgot you joined. A whale move that everyone in the know saw coming.

> The alpha is out there. You just can't be in 20 places at once. Until now."

---

## INTRODUCE BRAINIAC (0:35 – 0:55)

*Show the Brainiac dashboard loading — clean, dark, instant*

> "This is Brainiac. Your personal intelligence layer for Web3. Connect your Discord servers, your Telegram groups, your wallets — and Brainiac reads everything so you don't have to.

> Not a bot. Not a browser extension. Your actual intelligence hub, running 24/7."

---

## FEATURE: UNIFIED FEED (0:55 – 1:20)

*Navigate to Feed, messages appearing from multiple sources with AI tags*

> "Every message from every community you care about — Discord and Telegram — in one live feed. Brainiac's AI reads each one and tags it: is this alpha, a warning, an announcement, or just noise?

> You can filter by source, by tag, or just let the smart feed surface what actually matters. No more context switching. No more missed drops."

---

## FEATURE: AI BRIEFING (1:20 – 1:45)

*Open Brain tab, show Qwen AI chat responding to a question*

> "But aggregating messages is just the start. Ask Brainiac anything. 'What's everyone saying about this token?' 'Any wallet warnings in the last 24 hours?' 'Summarize the alpha from my three most active servers.'

> It's powered by Qwen — real reasoning, not keyword matching. Like having a research analyst who's been reading every chat for you all day."

---

## FEATURE: WALLET INTELLIGENCE (1:45 – 2:05)

*Navigate to Wallets page, show connected EVM + Solana wallets with activity*

> "Connect your EVM and Solana wallets and Brainiac correlates on-chain activity with what the community is talking about. If a wallet you're watching just made a move, and that same project is blowing up in your Telegram groups — you'll know.

> Context. That's what makes the difference between signal and noise."

---

## WEB3 NATIVE: AUTH + PAYMENTS (2:05 – 2:25)

*Show Privy sign-in, then the Upgrade page with crypto payment option*

> "Everything here is self-custodied. Sign in with Google, X, or your wallet — no passwords. Your Telegram sessions and Discord tokens live in your browser only. We never see them.

> And when you're ready to upgrade? No Stripe, no card details. You send crypto directly from your Brainiac wallet to ours. Wallet to wallet. That's it."

---

## ON-CHAIN LOGIN HISTORY (2:25 – 2:35)

*Show the 0G Newton testnet login history in the profile panel*

> "Every login is recorded on the 0G Newton testnet. Verifiable, permanent, and yours. No centralized auth server that can disappear overnight."

---

## CLOSE (2:35 – 2:50)

*Pull back to the dashboard overview*

> "Twenty groups. Zero missed opportunities.

> This is Brainiac — your edge in a world that moves faster than you can read. Sign up at brainiacai.co. It's free to start."

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- **Tone**: Natural, like a founder talking to a friend — not a corporate explainer.
- **Pacing**: Pause after the hook line. Let it land before the next sentence.
- **Music**: Subtle, low-BPM ambient. Nothing that competes with the voiceover.
- **B-roll**: Real app UI at each section — no mockup slides. Show the actual product.
- **Length**: ~2 min 50 sec. Cut from the bottom if needed; the hook through Features is the essential core.
